Main slide

1. The easiest move in judo can be learned in 10 minutes.

2. Judo is a Japanese grappling style focussing on throws and takedowns as well as chokes and joint locks.

3. The only striking techniques in judo are during kata practice which involves practicing pre-arranged patterns with a partner. No strikes are allowed in free practice or in competition.
